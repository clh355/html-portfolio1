The Green Mages Free Company

What we do:

  • Mount-farming, Treasure Maps, festive celebrations, Social events with prizes and more!
  • A big monthly FC social event with puzzles, competitions, races, and lots of prizes.
  • Clearing and farming current and old content. We do regular mount and achievement runs, Blue Mage activities, Extremes and Savages.
  • Regular play groups for roulettes, levelling, tomestone events and fun
  • Patient mentors willing to offer advice any time of day
  • Helpful members for shortening those queues throughout your adventure

Come down to the Mists and hangout at our large guildhall themed FC house.

We have an active Discord, where you can keep in contact with your fellow Green Mages, ask any questions you may have, or just be part of the family!

What we are looking for:

  • Friendly players to join our already warm and welcoming community
  • Players who are looking for help or are happy to help one another out
  • Socially active players that are willing to get involved, chat in FC chat and on discord
